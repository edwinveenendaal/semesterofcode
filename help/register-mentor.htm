Registration help for Mentors

 

Note: First and foremost, you must check that your organisation has already been registered in the system. You can check this by looking at the current list of organisations on the front page.

If your organisation is not already listed then you as the first person from your organisation must also assume the role of Organisation Administrator. See this link.

Otherwise you must request your existing organisations join up code from one of your organisation administrators. (If this information is not publically accessible, then ask on the mailing list here.)

How to register in the Semester of Code Virtual Placement System

Navigate to the Semester of Code VPS (http://vps2.semesterofcode.com) and click on Create new account at the bottom lefthand side of the screen.

Fill the information fields of the form with the data and information about your organisation. In the field Type the key you got in the invitation, use the key you were sent. Once you have submitted the form, you should receive an email containing an activation link. Copy the link into a browser, where you will be asked to set your password.
