How can I participate?

 

To enquire about joining the Semester of Code as either a new organisation or academic institution, you need will need to join the following mailing list.

https://www.jiscmail.ac.uk/cgi-bin/webadmin?A0=VALS-SOC

Send an email to the list introducing yourself and describing what you wish to achieve. You will be sent a register code which will allow you to self register in the system. If you want more information on how to register, you can find it in the section Registering yourself of the role that fits you as listed in the table of contents here.

Students can participate only by being invited by someone from an existing institution within the system. It is the responsibility of your academic supervisor to invite you by email and give you the code to signup.
