Your accepted project

 

After you have accepted your project a new option should appear in your dashboard area, 'My project'.

To access the resources for your accepted project click this link from now on. (Notice the 'Manage your project offers' link has now disappeared)

Here you now have two options. The first allows you to edit your optional project agreement.

Click the edit button at the top right corner to make amendments if needed. You can also set it as finished by clicking the student checkbox while in edit mode.

The second option from your 'My accepted project page' allows you to browse the project and proposal details.

You can also use this view to add comments/communicate with your supervisor and mentor as the comments are also emailed to both.
