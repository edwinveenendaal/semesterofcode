Registration help for students

 

How to register yourself as student

Note: You must first obtain a code to register yourself in the system. If you do not yet have one, you must request one from your academic supervisor. You will not be able to register if you do not have a code.

Navigate to the Semester of Code VPS (http://vps2.semesterofcode.com) and click on Create new account at the bottom lefthand side of the screen.

Fill the information fields of the form with your information. In the field Type the key you got in the invitation, use the key you were sent. Once you have submitted the form, you should receive an email containing an activation link. Copy the link into a browser, where you will be asked to set your password.

Your account should be automatically linked to your academic institution once created. Take a look around once logged in and visit the 'dashboard' link on the left, as well as the projects list.
